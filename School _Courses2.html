State Board Curriculum for 8th, 9th & 10th Standard

8th Standard State Board

Subjects: Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, Hindi/Marathi, Computer Science

Mathematics: Rational Numbers, Linear Equations, Geometry

Science: Metals & Non-Metals, Light, Sound, Electricity

Social Science: Indian History, Geography of India, Civics

9th Standard State Board

Subjects: Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, Hindi/Marathi, Computer Science

Mathematics: Polynomials, Probability, Geometry

Science: Atoms & Molecules, Motion, Work & Energy

Social Science: Indian Freedom Struggle, Constitution, World Geography

10th Standard State Board

Subjects: Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, Hindi/Marathi, Computer Science

Mathematics: Trigonometry, Quadratic Equations, Statistics

Science: Chemical Reactions, Magnetic Effects of Current, Life Processes

Social Science: Indian Economy, World Wars, Political Science
